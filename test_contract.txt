EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT

This Employment Contract is entered into between TechCorp Kenya Ltd (the "Company") and John Doe (the "Employee") on January 15, 2025.

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
The Employee shall serve as Software Developer and shall perform duties as assigned by the Company.

2. SALARY
The Employee shall receive a monthly salary of KES 150,000 payable monthly on the last working day of each month.

3. PROBATION PERIOD
The Employee shall serve a probation period of 6 months from the start date during which either party may terminate employment with 7 days notice.

4. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this contract with 30 days written notice. The Company may terminate immediately for cause including misconduct, breach of contract, or poor performance.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Employee agrees to maintain confidentiality of all company information, trade secrets, and proprietary data both during and after employment.

6. WORKING HOURS
Normal working hours are 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday, with a one-hour lunch break.

7. BENEFITS
The Employee is entitled to 21 days annual leave, medical insurance, and statutory benefits as per Employment Act 2007.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This contract is governed by the laws of Kenya and subject to Employment Act 2007 and other applicable Kenyan employment legislation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement on the date first written above.

Company: TechCorp Kenya Ltd
Employee: John Doe
